Impact on Edgehill

Proposed Variance – How It Harms Our Homes

A developer is seeking a variance to add a third story to an already non-conforming building that is less than 10 feet from our property line at 10 Edgehill—far below the required 30-foot setback.

If approved, this variance would:

  • Increase the building height by 12 feet, casting even more shadow over our backyard and home.
  • Add more windows directly facing our yard, further reducing privacy for us and our neighbors.
  • Set a dangerous precedent for more oversized, out-of-scale developments in our community.

We urge the Planning Board to reject this variance request to protect our privacy, sunlight, and neighborhood character.
